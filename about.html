Who I Am

I am a UX/UI designer with strong foundations in user research and design — from personas and journey mapping through to wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing. I design desktop and mobile interfaces in Figma and iterate based on real user feedback.

My background in web design and development means I understand what is technically feasible and build with accessibility in mind from the start. Every project I take on moves through a full user-centred process: research shapes the design, the design drives the prototype, and testing validates the build.

I am currently building my portfolio and looking for UX/UI design roles where I can apply that end-to-end process to real problems.
